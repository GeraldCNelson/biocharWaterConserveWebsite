Custom Growing-Season Periods

The Main Data Display and Summary Results tabs provide results at standard temporal aggregations—15 minutes, hourly, daily, and monthly. In this “Custom Season” tab you can edit or add your own sub-periods within a growing‐season (which may span across years).

Change the name, start or end date of any period below, or click “+ Add Period” to insert additional ones. These will then be available to use when you switch back to the other tabs.
