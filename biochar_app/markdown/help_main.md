## Directions for use of the options in the Interactive Plots tab

The control panel (left side) has the following choices

- Toggle between metric and US units

- Use the dropdowns to choose year, variable, strip, logger location, and depth.

- Click "Update Plots" to refresh the Raw and Ratio plots after changing a drop down.

- Drag a rectangle on the top plot to zoom in on a smaller time window. The ratio plot will automatically be synced.

- Use the "Download Data" and "Download Raw and Ratio Plots" menus to export what you see.

Item-specific help is available in the circle with an i in the middle.
