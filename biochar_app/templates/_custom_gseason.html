Custom Seasons

The Interactive Plots and Summary Results tabs provide results at standard temporal aggregations — every 15 minutes, hourly, daily, and monthly. This tab allows you to build your own aggregations to particular seasonal times. These can extend from before the anchor year to after it. The seasonal window can have multiple periods as shown below with some default values.

You can modify the time windows and period names below or just leave them as is. You can also add new periods. In the next two tabs, you can choose an aggregation to view, either a standard aggregation or the custom-built aggregation defined below.
