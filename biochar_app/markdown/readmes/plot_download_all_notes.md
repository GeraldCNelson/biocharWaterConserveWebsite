Plot Download Notes - All
-------------------------

Contents

- All downloads include raw logger columns plus matching ratio or comparison columns when available.
- Raw columns may include multiple depths or multiple logger locations depending on the plot grouping.
- Ratio columns are limited to the selected sensor depth.
- Temperature ratio columns may be omitted because temperature ratios are not used.

Column naming notes

- Raw columns describe individual strip, logger-location, and sensor-depth measurements.
- Ratio or difference columns describe paired strip comparisons.
