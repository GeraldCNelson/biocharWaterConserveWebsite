Custom Growing-Season Periods

The Main Data Display and Summary Results tabs provide results at standard temporal aggregations — every 15 minutes, hourly, daily, and monthly. This tab allows you to build your own aggregation period, called here the growing season which can extend from before the anchor year to after it. The growing season can have multiple periods as shown below with some default values.

You can modify the time windows and period names below or just leave them as is. You can also add new periods. In the next two tabs, you can choose an aggregation to view, either a standard aggregation or the custom-built aggregation defined below.
